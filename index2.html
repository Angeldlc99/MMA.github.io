About


Mission

We are a company dedicated to the recycling and smelting of non-ferrous metals, which provides integral solutions for the industrial sector, through innovative and high-tech processes, timely deliveries and achieving the required quality specifications, in order to generate value to our customers.

Vision

Modern Metal Alloys is an organization in constant growth, profitable, reliable and recognized for its quality, good business practices, competitiveness and innovation in the industrial environment.
